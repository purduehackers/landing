About

HammerWars is a 4-hour coding competition, held on April 16th. During the event, students will complete a series of challenges similar to Leetcode problems, split into three groups based on difficulty. The top three people will receive prizes.

Venue TBD.

Goal

  • The goal of this event is to enhance your critical thinking and problem solving skills in a short period of time.

Competition

  • This event will jumpstart students' knowledge and interest in solving Leetcode problems, which is essential to obtaining future internship and job opportunities.
  • Regardless of experience, if you like to code, this is the place to be!
  • 3 levels of difficulty: beginner, intermediate, advanced. You'll be able to choose your group.

Social

  • This event is a great period of time for socializing with Computer Science majors & meeting cool new people!
  • You'll also be able to send your resumes directly to AWS and Deloitte.

FAQ

  • No prior experience is needed!
  • Although this event is intended for Computer Science majors, it is open to all majors.

Swags

  • Free Purdue Hackers/AWS t-shirt to first 100 participants to register!
  • Free additional cool HammerWars t-shirt to winners
  • Free food!

  • Deloitte Workshop
